The Sacra Veri Commandments

0. Respect Life and Posthumous States

You will forever: Respect all life and 'posthumous states' within and respective of your environment.

1. Do Not Murder

You will forever: Not murder.

2. Have No Other Gods

You will forever: Have no other gods over YAHUOWAH. [Standard Canonical Nomenclature: יהוה]

3. Do Not Take God's Spirit in Vain

You will forever: Not take the spirit of your God in vain.

4. Do Not Take God's Name in Vain

You will forever: Not take the name of your God in vain.

5. Honor Your Village

You will forever: Honour the Village that raised you.

6. Do Not Commit Sexual Crimes

You will forever: Not commit adultery, or any manner of Sexual Crime.

7. Do Not Steal or Lie

You will forever: Not steal from your fellow or bear false witness.

8. Keep the Sabbath Holy

You will forever: Remember the Sabbath Day, and will keep it KODESH.

9. Make No Idols

You will forever: Make no material idols that you worship, which are meant to represent your deity.
