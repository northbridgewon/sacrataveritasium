THE SACRATA VERITASIUM COMMANDMENTS

Respect Life, Mortal Conditions, & Never Sell Creatures

You will forevermore: Respect all life and 'postmortem conditions' within and respective of your environment and never sell live creatures or their souls.

Do Not Murder or Intentionally Harm Others

You will forevermore: Not murder or intentionally harm other creatures.

Worship No Other Gods

You will forevermore: Worship no other gods other than ʏᴀɪᴇʜᴜᴏᴡᴀʜ. [Standard Font: YAIEHUOWAH]

Do Not Take Gods' Name or Spirit in Vain

You will forevermore: Not take the name or spirit of your God in vain.

Protect and Preserve the Cosmos

You will forevermore: Ensure the cosmos remains a pristine and sustainable domain for all life forms.

Honour Yourself and Society

You will forevermore: Honour yourself by taking good care of your mind and body, and honour the society that raised you by always trying your best.

Do Not Commit Sexual Crimes

You will forevermore: Not commit adultery, or any manner of Sexual Crime.

Do Not Steal or Lie

You will forevermore: Not steal from other creatures or bear false witness.

Rest One Day Per Week

You will forevermore: Put aside one day a week, dedicating that day to self rediscovery and spiritual growth.

Make No Idols

You will forevermore: Make no material idols that you worship, which are meant to represent your god.
